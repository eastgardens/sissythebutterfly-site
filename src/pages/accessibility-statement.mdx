We want every family to be able to enjoy Sissy the Butterfly — including visitors who use
screen readers, keyboards, magnification, captions, or reduced motion.

## What we do

- This site is built on an accessibility-first foundation: semantic HTML, landmark regions,
  a skip link, logical headings, and full keyboard navigation with visible focus indicators.
- Every image that carries meaning has a text description; decorative images are hidden from
  screen readers.
- Colors aim for WCAG AA contrast, and the site respects your system's dark-mode and
  reduced-motion preferences.
- Our printables are made to be printed and enjoyed off-screen — no account, no app, no
  time limits.

## Where we're honest

We're a small team — a teacher, her family, and carefully supervised helpers — and we're
always improving. If something on this site doesn't work well for you, that's a bug we want
to fix.

## Tell us

Email **sissythebutterfly3@gmail.com** and a real person will read it. Please tell us what
happened and, if you can, which browser or assistive technology you were using.
